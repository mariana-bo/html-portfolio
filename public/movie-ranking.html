The Best Movies According to Mariana

My top 3 movies of all time.

Life Is Beautiful

This is a heartbreaking but beautiful movie. The mix of comical elements in the midst of the tragic events occurring is very well done.

Parasite

The uncomfortableness I felt when watching this movie in the cinema is one I will never forget. The plot, the visual and sound effects, the acting and the social critic behind it, all make this movie completely worth watching.

A Man Called Otto

This movie made me sob. This is a movie made by the characters and their interactions and emotions and not by a crazy plot. I really liked it.
